St. Louis

What did I do?

  • Explored the city
  • Traveled up the Gateway Arch
  • Went to a Cardinals game

Entry: St. Louis was a great start to my trip! I faced my fear of heights on the Gateway Arch, and saw a beautiful view from the top! Then we went to a Cardinals game where I had the worst cheeseburger I have ever had! It turns out it was because the cheese was beer cheese . Despite the gross beer cheese, St. Louis was such a fun city, and I would love to go back!

Pros Cons
Beautiful view from Gateway Arch Beer cheese was disgusting
Baseball game was super entertaining Food is pricey
Interesting history a little sketchy at night

Sedona

What did I do?

  • Pink Jeep Tours
  • Crystal Shopping
  • Held snakes

Entry: Sedona was certainly in my top three favorite stops on the trip! I had so much fun shopping for crystals (I eventually found a beautiful blue crystal called Apatite ). I also got to hold a King snake called Orca! We also travelled throughout the mountains of red rocks in pink jeeps! It was so fun speeding over the bumpy roads, while learning about Sedona's fascinating history!

Pros Cons
Mystical vibes Extremely HOT
Beautiful scenery Crowded
Unique Javalenas will haunt you in your sleep

Malibu

What did I do?

  • Created Pepto Pigsmol
  • Watched the Sunset
  • Helped out with chores

Entry: After creating a masterpiece of a drawing, Pepto Pigsmol, we arrived just in time in Malibu to watch the sunset. It was the most vibrant sunset I had ever seen! The entire sky was colored with various shades of pink. We then headed to a campsite that was so close to the ocean, you could hear the waves crash as you fell asleep. Before the night ended I went around and helped everyone complete their shifts. Even though the cooking, and dish washing was not fun on its own, I am so grateful that it gave me an opportunity to get to know the other participants. Malibu definitely has a special vibe to it!

Pros Cons
Best sunsets Scary bathrooms
Favorite Campsite Lots of traffic
Beautiful drive Not many stores
