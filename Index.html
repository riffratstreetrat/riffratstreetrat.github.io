My TCTW Experience!

TCTW is a 23 day experience that takes 50 teenagers, across the country. While on this journey to California, the participants stop in 23 states, and many beautiful sites. The group travels to cities like Sedona, Chicago, and Las Angelos, and National Parks like Yellowstone, the Grand Canyon, and Yosemite. On this three week adventure the 50 strangers you began with grow to become a new family.
